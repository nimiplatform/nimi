# Economy Contract

> Domain: economy
> Rule family: R

## Scope

This contract defines economy constraints adjacent to the Realm core.

## R-ECON-001

World creator economy and access economics remain auditable, explicit, and separate from narrative runtime.

## R-ECON-002

AI compute route cost is not modeled as Realm core truth or hidden world mutation.

## R-ECON-003

Revenue and settlement logic use explicit event types and share plans with append-only accounting.

## R-ECON-004

Apps cannot hide economic state changes inside narrative history or memory commits.
