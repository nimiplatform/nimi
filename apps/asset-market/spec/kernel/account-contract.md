# Account Contract — AM-ACCOUNT-*

> Minimal account-facing records for the market app.

## AM-ACCOUNT-001: Minimal Scope

Account is present because Asset Market is a market application, but it remains intentionally light.

Current minimum scope:

- account identity information
- acquisition records
- published package records

## AM-ACCOUNT-002: No Heavy Merchant Console

Current account scope excludes:

- advanced settlement workflows
- invoice/tax tooling
- review moderation consoles
- detailed merchant analytics

Those remain future expansion concerns.
