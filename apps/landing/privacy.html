Last updated: March 11, 2026

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect limited categories of information such as:

We use information to operate, secure, and improve the public Nimi experience, including:

The landing site may use standard browser storage, cookies, or analytics-related technologies for language selection, performance measurement, and site reliability. If enabled, these tools are used in a limited way consistent with operating a public software site.

Links from the site may take you to GitHub, documentation services, provider resources, or other third-party properties. Those services operate under their own privacy policies, and Nimi does not control their practices.

5. Data retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for security, operational, analytics, or legal purposes, unless a longer retention period is required by law or legitimate business need.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information associated with the public site. No internet transmission or storage system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your choices

You can control certain browser-based privacy settings, including cookie preferences, local storage, and standard browser tracking controls. You may also avoid submitting optional information through contact or feedback channels.

8. Updates

We may revise this policy as the site, product distribution model, or legal requirements evolve. When we do, the revised date on this page will be updated.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions about the public Nimi site, contact Nimi Network Limited through the official channels linked on the landing page or project repository.
